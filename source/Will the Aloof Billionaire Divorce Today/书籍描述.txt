Orphaned Scarlett Yates lost her parents at an early age and was unexpectedly bound by a childhood engagement to Matthew Saxon, the young master of a top wealthy family. Matthew, who advocates free love, strongly rejects this arranged marriage. He even sends people to forcibly take Scarlett to his luxury mansion out of full resistance.
Scarlett is shocked and angry at being taken away like a kidnapping in broad daylight. When she meets Matthew, a cold-tempered and arrogant young master, she is neither timid nor submissive. A sealed family marriage contract ties the rebellious CEO and the stubborn orphan girl tightly together.
Bound by force and starting off on bad terms, they bicker and interact under the same roof. As misunderstandings fade away, hidden feelings grow quietly. The cold and domineering Matthew gradually falls for Scarlett and gives her exclusive indulgence. A sweet love story destined by an arranged marriage begins.

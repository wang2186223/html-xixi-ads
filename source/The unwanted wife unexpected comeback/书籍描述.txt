On their second wedding anniversary, Alicia is drugged by her husband Joshua. By accident, she spends one passionate night with the mysterious tycoon Caden, leaving money and slipping away without learning his identity. Joshua has long loved another woman Lilliana, who is pregnant with his child. He always looks down on his plain wife, convinced Alicia will cling to him forever. After the unexpected night, Alicia wakes up and sees through Joshua’s cold cruelty, making up her mind to file for a divorce. Caden, who falls for her after their one-night encounter, finds out her marital status and quietly keeps an eye on her. Having once drained all her savings to help the penniless Joshua build his career, Alicia cuts ties without hesitation. Powerful Caden walks into her life with sincere affection, while Joshua sinks into endless regret after losing her and tries in vain to win her back.

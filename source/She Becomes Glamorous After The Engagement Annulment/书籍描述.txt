At nineteen, Nora Smith woke up from a severe illness only to be struck by a "thunderclap" of news: she was four months pregnant. Despite having never been intimate with anyone and surveillance proving she had stayed home for months, the world remained skeptical and mocked her. Under the cold scolding of her father and the disdain of her fiancé, Anthony Gray, she was forced to give birth.
Five years later, Nora returns to the States with her daughter, Cherry. No longer the "obese" woman she once was due to childhood hormonal medication, she has transformed into a breathtaking beauty and hides her identity as the world-renowned surgeon, "Anti". While her public reason for returning is to annul her engagement with the Grays, her true mission is to find her son—the other twin who was forcibly taken by her father at birth. Little does she know, her path is about to collide with Justin Hunt, the powerful head of the nation's top family.

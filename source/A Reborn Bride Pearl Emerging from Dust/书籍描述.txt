On their wedding day, Gideon abandoned Calliope for his true love.After the wedding, he spent every night away from home.During her birthday kidnapping, he scolded her and continued to pursue his love.Gasping for help, her family turned a blind eye, too busy planning a party for their adopted daughter. In their eyes, her death was justified.Only the powerful and influential man held her lifeless body, guarding her for days and nights.When given a miraculous second chance at life, she marries the man.As their love unfolds, she rebuilds her life, emerging from the dust and captivating all, including those who had once forsaken her.Panic ensues as Gideon discovers his true savior was her, not his lover.Her parents discover she wasn't an unlucky star, but their adopted daughter was.Her eldest brother finally realizes she -his biological sister- cured his legs, not his adopted sibling.Her other brother learns it was her who saved his life, not his adoptive sister.Her third brother finds out, without her, he's bullied worldwide in the entertainment industry.Their entire family starts to regret their actions...

Dying on a cold operating table, abandoned by the husband she loved with all her heart—that was Sophie Russell's first life. Bleeding out after childbirth, her final call for James Burke was met with chilling indifference: "Call me when she's gone."

Then she opens her eyes.

She is twenty-two again, newly married to Devonport's most powerful tycoon. But this time, Sophie remembers everything: the years of neglect, the humiliation of playing second fiddle to his true love Nichole, and the devastating end that awaits her.

James expects the same desperate, love-blind wife who copies Nichole's white dresses and begs for his attention. Instead, he gets a woman in a stunning red gown who looks through him like a stranger. At a high-stakes auction, Sophie makes a shocking move—spending ten billion on a worthless piece of land—not for James, but for her own future. When the mysterious and dangerous billionaire Colby steps into the game, Sophie finds herself caught between two powerful men. But she isn't playing for love anymore. She's playing for survival. And in this life, she will never be anyone's fool again.

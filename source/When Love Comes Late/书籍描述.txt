To honor her grandfather’s final wish, Stella hastily married a man she had never met. Despite being legally wed, their lives remained completely separate, with barely any interaction. A year later, Stella returned to Seamarsh City, determined to finally meet her elusive husband. To her surprise, she received a message from him—requesting a divorce, though they had never met in person. Angrily, Stella agreed: “Fine, let’s divorce!” In a bold move, Stella joined the Prosperity Group, taking on the role of a public relations officer working directly for the enigmatic CEO, Matthew. Known for his striking looks and unwavering devotion to his unseen wife, Matthew’s personal life was a mystery. Little did Stella know, her mysterious husband was none other than Matthew, leading a double life! Focused on her career, Stella kept her distance from Matthew, even as she noticed his subtle efforts to draw closer to her. But as time passed, her husband had a change of heart and refused to proceed with the divorce. When would his secret identity be revealed?

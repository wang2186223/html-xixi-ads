Lydia Smith, a rural herbalist, was livestreaming herb-collecting in the stormy mountains when she accidentally saved Connor Jefferson, a mysterious man who was bitten by a venomous snake and on the verge of death. She was skilled, calm and indifferent, only wanting to get her medical fee and leave after saving him, unwilling to get involved with a man of complicated status.But Connor was not only a billionaire tycoon, but also deeply attracted by her frankness and medical skills. An accidental lip lock entangled their fates completely. He wanted to repay her and keep her by his side with everything he had, while she only wanted to stay away from troubles; he fell step by step and loved her deeply, while she kept a low profile and lived quietly.When a rural miracle doctor meets a cold CEO, an extreme indulgence and two-way redemption starting with a life-saving favor begins.

After twenty years as a daughter of the wealthy Gill family, Harlee Sanderson is cast aside the moment their biological daughter, Adelina, returns. Stripped of her name, her place, and her future, she is sent back to her impoverished birth family with nothing but a hundred dollars and the sneers of those who once called her "sister."

But Adelina's victory is built on lies. When she tries to frame Harlee for stealing her fashion sketchbook, Harlee fights back—exposing Adelina's designs as cheap copies of famous brands. Humiliated but not defeated, Adelina watches Harlee walk away... then watches in shock as Harlee climbs into a custom-modified helicopter and flies off into the night.

Because Harlee Sanderson is no ordinary discarded daughter. She is a genius engineer, a skilled pilot, and a woman with secrets that could shake the foundations of high society. When she crosses paths with Rhys Green—a powerful heir whose grandfather lies dying—she saves a life and earns a debt of honor. Now, as she returns to the slums to find her true family, Harlee is about to discover that the blood she came from may be worth more than all the gold of her former home.

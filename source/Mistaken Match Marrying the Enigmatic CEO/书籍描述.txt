Emmanuel arrives at a blind date in a café, but he's late because he went to the wrong place. The woman at the table is annoyed by his tardiness and criticizes his low income. She even splashes water on him and leaves. As Emmanuel exits the café, he witnesses a staged car accident and sexual harassment involving a woman. He steps in to help and exposes the thugs' lies. Meanwhile, Mackenzie, who owns the café, observes the scene and is impressed by Emmanuel's bravery.

Later, Emmanuel's mother, Alessandra, is frustrated with his unsuccessful blind dates and decides to visit the woman from the latest one. Emmanuel is reluctant and embarrassed by his mother's insistence...

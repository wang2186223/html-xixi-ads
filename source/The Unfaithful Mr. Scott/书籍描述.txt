Minnie has stayed by Eugene’s side for years, devoting all her affection yet remaining nothing more than his secret lover. After a banquet, Eugene dotes heavily on new intern Vi: he personally buys her milk and candies, specially transfers her to the president’s office and warns Minnie to leave the girl alone. He ignored Minnie’s injuries in the past but cannot bear to see the innocent Vi get wronged. Witnessing his blatant favoritism, Minnie finally sees through their lopsided bond and chooses to let go. Eugene keeps protecting the docile Vi while still clinging to Minnie’s company. As days pass, he gradually realizes his genuine love lies with Minnie, only to find she has already walked out of his life when he tries to win her back.

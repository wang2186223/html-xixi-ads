After three years of a contractual marriage, Evadne (Na) poured ten years of unrequited love into waiting humbly for her husband Thaddeus, yet never melted his cold heart. When his beloved Acacia returned home, Thaddeus couldn’t wait to file for divorce on Evadne’s birthday. He intended to send her off casually with two million dollars and a villa, eager to marry his true love.
Heartbroken, Evadne signed the divorce papers and left without hesitation. No one knew this ordinary, helpless country girl was actually the heiress of the top-tier Ashbourne family. Dropping her humble disguise, she returned to her real identity, embracing family power and endless favor.
It was only after driving away the one who truly loved him that Thaddeus woke up with deep regret. He realized he had abandoned her on her birthday, and was shocked to discover her noble background. He tried desperately to win her back, only to be met with her cold indifference. Trapped in endless remorse, he could only linger in memories, while Evadne lived a brilliant new life cherished by her family.

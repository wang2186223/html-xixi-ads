Kallie Cooper lost her voice to a childhood illness, but she never lost her ability to love—even when that love was given to Jake Reeves, a husband who married her only out of obligation. For five years, she has been the perfect, silent wife: making his breakfast, enduring his indifference, and swallowing her tears as he carries on an affair with his true love, Sarah Miller.

When Jake's family humiliates Kallie at a celebration, mocking her inability to bear children, Jake surprisingly defends her. For one fleeting moment, Kallie dares to hope. But that hope shatters when Sarah appears—clinging to Jake, wiping away Kallie's very presence from the passenger seat, and reducing her to nothing but an obstacle.

Falsely accused of attacking Sarah and locked out of her own home during a thunderstorm, Kallie collapses in the rain. When she wakes, she makes a decision that shocks everyone: she wants a divorce. But Jake, who never wanted her, suddenly refuses to let her go. Now Kallie must find the strength to fight for her freedom—and discover if the man who silenced her heart can ever learn to hear it.

Chapter 87

Charlotte wanted to call for help, but the road was filled with speeding cars and there were hardly any pedestrians.

Charlotte didn't know what was happening to her, she just felt it was getting harder and harder to breathe, and a greater sense of panic rose in her heart.

Could it be that she was going to die here?

Tears streamed down her face, and Charlotte didn't have time to regret it,forcing herself to breathe as steadily as possible.

She had to save herself, even if it was for the baby in her belly.

With difficulty, she took out her phone and instinctively wanted to call Frederick, but thinking about the situation just now, Charlotte believed he wouldn't answer her call at this time.

Without much hesitation, Charlotte called Jasper.

"Hello? Charlotte." Jasper answered almost immediately.

Charlotte tried to breathe steadily, but could only say brokenly, "I... can't breathe, my hands and feet are numb, what should I do?"

Charlotte tried to describe her symptoms as briefly as possible, but the numb and cold feeling was already spreading from the ends of her limbs.

"Don't be afraid, try to calm yourself down and stay calm." Jasper put down his work and guided Charlotte step by step to save herself, "Is there a plastic bag or paper bag around you?" "No." Charlotte was trying to control her fear, but she was still crying.

"Okay, it's fine. Now use your other hand to cover your mouth and nose completely, try to cover it tightly. Don't be afraid, your symptoms are just respiratory alkalosis. As long as you cover it and reduce the exhalation of carbon dioxide, it will slowly alleviate." Jasper's voice was gentle yet strong.which made Charlotte's uneasy emotions calm down a lot.

Charlotte tried to cover her mouth and nose, leaving only a small gap for air to pass through.

"Yes,you're doing great. Keep it up, the symptoms will disappear soon.This is not a serious condition, you were just too emotional just now.As long as you can retain the carbon dioxide, you'll be fine."

Sure enough, just as Jasper said, Charlotte soon felt her breathing was no longer so rapid, and she was no longer gasping for ai,but her body was still numb and weak.

"When you feel a bit better, tell me where you are, and I'll come find you,"Jasper said as he got up and started changing clothes.

The pregnancy hormones might make her more sensitive, but they wouldn't cause respiratory alkalosis for no reason. Charlotte must have been strongly stimulated to become so emotional.

No matter where she was, he had to find her as soon as possible.

Charlotte felt very touched.

Her breathing had gradually stabilized. "But I don't know where I am."

Because the party was held in a villa, it was on the outskirts of the city,not a place she usually went to.

"Okay, no problem. Let's hang up first, and you send me your location."Jasper held his car keys and went downstairs, ready to find her as soon as she sent her location.

"Okay," Charlotte responded.

After hanging up the phone, Charlotte realized she had somehow slid off the stone block and was now sitting on the ground, her back against the stone block.

She felt a bit fortunate. If it weren't for the stone block by the roadside, she might not even have the strength to sit up straight.

After catching her breath for a while, Charlotte finally had a bit of strength and was about to send her locátion to Jasper when the light in front of her was suddenly blocked.

Charlotte looked up and saw two men dressed in shabby clothes, their eyes full of amazement and desire.

She wanted to get up, but she had no strength at all.

"Go away!" Charlotte shouted with all her might, trying to sound fierce.

But in her current weak state, even if she tried to act tough, she looked like a toothless kitten.

"Quite a temper, I like it." One of the men, with a lewd smile and yellow teeth, said, "You don't need to be so scared. We're not bad guys. We saw you sitting here alone from a distance, so we came over to see if you needed any help."

"No need! My friend will be here soon to pick me up." Charlotte tried to keep a stern face and hold herself up.

If she didn't act tough now, the other party would only hurt her more carelessly. So she had to be strong now.

"You've got quite a temper, woman. My brother is gentle with women,but I don't play that gentlemanly game. You'd better cooperate with us, or you'Il suffer."

The red-haired man threatened Charlotte with a very bad attitude and then tried to pull her.

"Don't touch me! Get your dirty hands off me!" Charlotte tried to dodge,secretly unlocking her phone to call the police.

."You filthy bitch! Are you disgusted by me? I know you women are all the same,cheap and gold-digging! You throw yourselves at rich men, but act all high and mighty with us poor guys! You're all trash! Watch me ruin you today!"

The red-haired man,enraged,reached out to pull Charlotte's hair.

The yellow-toothed man stopped him, put his arm around his neck,and walked a few steps away, "Don't be impulsive! Did you forget what that person said..."

Charlotte couldn't hear what he said next, but she became suspicious.

Charlotte initially thought she was just unlucky to get out of the car early and run into thugs,but the yellow-toothed man mentioned 'that person,which meant someone might be behind this.

Charlotte's first thought was Serena because she hadn't offended anyone else.

No,there was also the Russell family... The last time theyhad such an unpleasant encounter, the Russell family might have wanted to take revenge on her.

Charlotte's mind was in turmoil, with various thoughts and guesses making her head hurt, but the most important thing now was to call the police.

Taking advantage of the two men whispering, Charlotte edited a distress message, but before she could send it, the red-haired man saw it.

He rushed over and snatched the phone from Charlotte's hand.

Charlotte had no strength to resist and could only watch him take it away.

"This filthy bitch wants to call the police! See, I told you we should have made her suffer first! Otherwise, she wouldn't behave."

The red-haired man waved the phone screen in front of the yellow-toothed man and then rushed over to slap Charlotte hard.

"You bitch! You're just like my gold-digging ex-girlfriend,all bitches who need to be taught a lesson!"

"Get up! I will fuck you today!"

Chapter 22

Talia's pov

"Okay so my name is Ken Wayne, I'm 27 years old little butterfly!" Keven smiled and I frown at another dumb nickname and the rest laughed.

'Nice to properly meet you Ken :D'

"You too little butterfly" he smirked and I frowned but had a smafl smile on my face.

He have short black hair, hazel brown eyes, straight nose and a sharp jaw.

"I'm Alan Bellard and I'm 26 years old little flower!" Alan said with another dumb nickname I just ended up facepalm myself at the nickname and shook my head with a smirk.

"I'm glad you are accepting our new names for you little flower" Alan smirked.

He have champagne brown hair, sea green eyes, button nose and sharp jawline.

Honestly everyone in this house I have seen till now is like 170+ cm tall and super muscular, they are all giant's compare to me, same with Kay.

'Well it's nice to meet you all and you four will be the only ones that is allowed to give mne such ridiculous nicknames xD Consider yourself special and super lucky!'

My four guards as well as Theo and Aaron both laughed at that.

"Okay let's go" they said and we all headed out. They guards got into one

car as me and Theo got into Aaron's Audi A8.

I got comfortable in the back and we were on the way to the mall.

During the ride I were pretty quiet, well can you blame me? It's pretty annoying to write everything.

When we got closer to the mall. Theo turned around from the passenger seat to look at me.

"Okay, I get you may not want us to buy everything, but we promised Leon to pamper you with everything you may need, so don't try to argue with us on anything, just let us buy whatever because we have 12 years of spoiling to do catch up on, so please promise us you will not interfere!"Theo said in a stern voice but also a smirk on his face.

I took a deep sigh and then nodded.

"I need you to write it down so we have on black and white" Theo said smirking and I just facepalmed myself again, way to use my mute situation to their advantage.

'I promise, I will let you pamper me how YOU TWO want and I will not argue or interfere in any way!

I wrote and turned the book for he to read it.

"Good, now hand me the page!" He said holding out his hand and I did as I were told.

"Thank you bambina, now time to show you how a princess is being taking care of!" He said before turning back around and I just let out a deep sigh which made both Theo and Aaron laugh.

We arrived at the mall and waited a few minutes for my guards then we started to head in. "So it will be interesting to see how much of a fight she will put up!" Jack said chuckling.

"Oh don't worry" Theo said and holding up my promise "She have promised not to do so" he smirked and my guards laughed.

"Good this will be fun, hope you are ready to drown in new clothes and other stuff your brother deem important." Charlie said laughing.

"Oh and if you guards alsosee something you think she may need please let us know!" Aaron said serious but also had a smirk on his face looking at my guards who all smiled brightly and nodded and I just let out another defeated sigh which everyone laughed at again.

First we got all my school supplies, then they started with clothes,when they walked into 'Victoria secrets' I kinda just stood there and just wanted the floor to swallow me whole.

But that didn't stop my brothers or guards to buy mne a ton of underwear.lingerie and pj and whatnot in here. I think they understood I were not comfortable atall so they let me just stand here, but they all kept their eye on me.

After they all had filled up one BIG bag EACH of stuff we headed out,Jack and Charlie went to the car with the stuff while me, Theo, Aaron,Ken and Alan went into another store and told them where we went so they would meet us there.

In the next store they did the same, They all just walked around, picked everything they think I would look good in, and I'm not gonna lie I feel they bought the entire store.

When we were done there and Charlie and Jack got back Ken and Alan went out with the new stuff we got.

And we got to the next store. And this were how things went on for a few hours.

We went into almost all thestores in the mall, they also got me the newest samsung, and laptop,a smartwatch.

They got me stuff for my hair, also all kind of tools for my hair.

At a makeup store a lady working there got me lots and lots of makeup.

We also got into a store to get some stuff for my room, a bookstore to get books, and more notebooks for me to write in.

After a while we got to the food court and the guys were hungry.

I just wanted a chicken sandwich also knowing I won't be able to eat all of it.

After they were all done eating we continued on this torturous journey through the mall, and as I promised I just kept my mouth shut and didn't interfere.

Then a few more hours later we finally got home and I were so exhausted.

Some of the guards stationed around at home as well as my guards and two brothers carried everything up to my room, and I were not allowed to carry anything.

Myself, Theo, Aaron and my guards used a few more hours to get everything sorted out. Now my closet is full of clothes, my bookshelf have some books, my vanity dešk have lots of makeup and stuff like that.

Charlie put up some of the pictures we got, my bathroom cabinets have gotten a lot of stuff in them too. A few blankets have been placed in my window seat, as well as some been placed in a basket on the floor next to my balcony door.

Theo is setting up my electronic devices for me to use. And the rest of us is just chilling talking and joking or of course I'm just writing to them.

Then when Theo is done we head down for dinner.

Let's just pray this will go better than yesterday's dinner.

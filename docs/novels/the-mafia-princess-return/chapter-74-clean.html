Chapter 74

Dominic's pov

"Well that sounds like a plan then!" I said with a smile looking at Talia in Dina's arm who just hugging him tightly.

"Sweetheart, may I ask for a favor from you?" Michelle asked and Talia nodded with a smile.

"Can we hear some piano playing from you, it's been so long since we have heard it" She asked and Talia looked over at me with a raised eyebrow.

"She is asking you for permission" Kayden said smiling at Talia.

"Oh of course you can, I think we all would love to hear the Bianchi princess play music" I said with a smile and you heard a lot happy cheers around the ballroom.

Dina let Talia down on the floor and when her feet hit the floor both Zeus and Hades stoodup, when Talia walked over to the piano they followed her, when she sat down on the piano stool they laid down on the floor next to her and we all moved a bit closer to her.

She started playing and man it sure were some beautiful piano music (my heart will go on (from "Titanie") - Piano version-Patrik Pietschmann)

I were just so lost in her musie and I think everyone else were too.

Then you could see some people started to dance to it.

Next she played (A thousand years - Piano version - Riyandi Kusuma)

Like L know I can play piano but she make it look more easier then it is.

She just sitting there with eyes closed and let the musie fly out of her fingers like she have not done anything else in her life.

"Oh,how I have missed hearing her play honey!" Michelle said leaning up against her husband Dina,

"Yeah it's so good to see our little princess again" He said holding around her.

I saw Kayden and his friends just look at her in awe.

Seemed like her and Kayden and his friends are close.

Well she sure deserve all the love in the world, she looks like a girl with a pure heart and she seem so innocent, sweet and kind.

Then she started to play (River flows in you-Yiruma)

"Wow she is good" I heard Nico say in shock.

"You guys haven't heard her play yer?" Dina asked shocked.

"Me. Linda and Kayden did when we saved her in Seattle" Jacob said looking at Talia with admiration in his eyes.

"We only have seen a picture of her playing from when they were with her in Seattle" Leon explained.

Then she played (Perfect - Piano version -Daniele Leoni)

"She have only been home twoweeks and t day, and the time have been filled with a lot of different stuff." Arthur explained.

"Wait Jacob you said 'saved her', what do you mean?" Dina asked worried.

"We can explain it all tomorrow for all of you!" Thomas said with a sad smile.

"Does this have something to do with why she don't talk?" Santiago asked and the Bianchi and Miller family all nodded their head.

Then she played (Right here waiting - Piano version -Riyandi Kusuma)

What have this poor girl been going through?

That she have stopped talking and had to be saved by the Millers.

"We also want to talk to you guys about potentially move into the Bianchi estate for a little at least, we have gotten some very valuable information about the Irish thanks to a certain princess." Leon whispered so only me and Santiago could hear it.

"Well we will move in without a question if we get to spend time with Talia" Santiago said with a smile and I nodded.

"And I think Dina and his wife would love to join as well" I said and they looked at me with bright smiles and nodded.

Then she played (The one that got away (piano version)- Riyandi Kusuma)

"Well we can help you start moving in tomorrow morning and have a formal dinner tomorrow night celebrating our alliance?" Leon suggested and we all nodded.

"But I think I need to bring those two with me if that is okay?" I said to Leon pointed at Zeus and Hades.

"Now that they have meet Talia again, you will have a hard time getting them away from her let me tell you!" Dina chuckle and Michelle smiled and nodded.

Next she played (Shivers - piano version-Flying fingers)

"I have a hard time believing they even will let her go home alone today.so maybe you should let them go home with her today?" Michelle said in a serious tone.

"As long as you promise they won't eat anyone or ruin our house I don't mind them at all" Arthur said smiling.

"Seem like Talia is also very fond of them." Josefine said.

I looked up and saw both Zeus and Hádes had sat down next to her and put their head on her lap.

After listen to a few more songs Talia were playing she closed the lid of the piano and stood up and she walked over to me and gave me a bow.

"Sweetheart why are you bowing to me we all should bow to you in thanks for a wonderful piano playing" I said with a big smile on my face.

I have never felt so at peace ever before as I am now listen to her playing.

"She is saying thank you for letting her using the piano" Theo came up to Talia and put a hand around her waist and gave her a side hug.

"Your very welcome sweetheart but still it's us that should thank you for the amazing music" I said with a smile and then I bowed down and everyone else in the ballroom did as well.

When I looked back up P'saw Talia had tears in her eyes but a smile on

her face.

I pulled her gently into a hug and she hugged me back and I could hear she started to cry.

And that broke my heart, what have this sweet wonderful human being been going through?

She pulled away from the hug and she put her hands on my cheeks then she wiped away some tears I didn't even noticed had fallen down my face.

I did the same for her as well very gently so I wouldn't mess up her makeup.

Then she walked over to Kayden and his friends and would you know it,Zeus and Hades followed her.

Suddenly I felt an arm around my shoulder and I looked to my sde and seeing my brother.

"I see my big brother have gotten soft" Niklas said and I just smiled.

"More that I feel a big brother protectiveness over her" I said smiling looking at Talia.

"You are not alone Dominic, from the second I meet her, both me and Stuart have felt the same!" Victor said.

"So now she have even more brothers that will protect her, starting to feel sorry for her" Santiago smirked and the rest of us laugh.

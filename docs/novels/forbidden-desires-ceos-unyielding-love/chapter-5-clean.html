Emily remained silent.

Emily was about to go when she saw Patrick's murderous look.

"Get moving," his tone was commanding.

Emily sighed and gave Marlon an apologetic look.

At the door of the private room, Shirley didn't rush in.

Shirley asked, "Emily,why are you dressed so sexy today?"

What could Emily say? That Patrick forced her to wear it?

Patrick said, "Get in here!"

As soon as they sat down, the client arrived.

"I've heard Mr. Rivera is always surrounded by beautiful women. Seeing it today.it's true."

Shirley clenched her fists in jealousy as the client's lecherous eyes roamed over Emily's barely concealed chest.

"Let's toast to our future cooperation!"

Patrick downed his drink, but the client didn't sit down.

"They say Emily is a rare beauty. Seeing her today, it's true. May I have the honor of a drink with you?"

The bald man leered at Emily.

Shirley said. "Emily istruly a star employee at our company. And her drinking capacity? I doubt anyone can match that!"

"Is that so?" The bald man left his seat and moved closer to Emily.

Emily was in a difficult position. The company valued this deal highly,or Patrick wouldn't be here in person.

"Absolutely, I'd be delighted."

Before Patrick could speak, Emily downed her drink.

After several rounds, she was feeling tipsy.

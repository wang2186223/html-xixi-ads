Chapter 7

Everyone liked Emily now, even though she never used to show up at any parties and she had been abandoned by the groom at her wedding.

Someone nearby said, "Ms. Emily Johnson isn't what the rumors say.She's smart and beautiful. Johnreally has bad taste, choosing Veda."

"It's no surprise, since a mistress knows how to seduce a man."

Veda heard this and her face turned pale. She stared at Emily, who was surrounded by people not far away. All of this should have been hers!Why did Emily get to take it all away from her?

She was about to get up and find John, ready to embarrass Emily in front of him. Even though Emily was elegant and noble, she couldn't even keep a man.

Sensing Veda's frustration, Mia quickly pressed her down and said, "Today is the Smith family's banquet, which is important for our famnily.Don't cause trouble. Mrs. Smith already has some opinions about you."

Veda blushed. "But why do they all want to be friends with Emily? Isn't she just good at making fans?"

Mia sighed, staring at Veda. After attending so mnany high-profile banquets with her, Veda still couldn't see through this. Mia could only remind her,"What they value is Emily's ability to please Mrs. Smith. Now,do you get it?11

Veda felt it was unfair, but she could only swallow her anger. Emily knew these people were untouchable, the children of high-ranking officials, so she could only add them on Facebook. Suddenly, a girl said, "He seems to be coming over today."

Then someone said, "I met him at Starry Capital before and exchanged contact info with him. He's really a gentle person."

"You're making it up. When did he ever interact with women?"

"He asked me out for dinner before. He's really handsome."

Someone followed up with a joke. "He's going to acquire your family's subsidiary company, so he meant to put pressure on you through the dinner."

"We had dinner together anyway. It's just a company. We'll give it to him if he wants it."

Emily's eyes flickered for a moment and she asked, "Ladies, are you talking about James?"

"Yes, he's even more handsome than male celebrities."

It turned out everyone valued appearance. But that person had the same name as her lover, so Emily felt a different meaning when she heard this.

James also loved coffee. Emily looked at the cup for a while, not listening to how the girls were teasing each other, and took a picture to send to James.

She sent a text message with the picture: [There's Blue Mountain coffee here. If you like it, I can help you drink more.] A long time passed and there was no reply. Emily continued to edit the message. But at that moment, the butler said, "Mr. Smith is back." Everyone looked up. Emily sent out the latest message. In a moment of silence, only her Facebook notification sound rang out, and she looked up in astonishment.

James had just entered the banquet hall. He was dressed in a well-fitted dark suit with a silver-gray tie, which complemented his deep eyes and added a touch of mystery.

But when Emily saw James' face, her whole body stiffened instantly.

Her brain buzzed, her hand holding the phone tightened slightly,fingertips turning white.

James' eyes were as cold as a deep-sea undercurrent.

With a slight lift of his eyebrows, it felt like the air got sucked out of the room.James'gaze swept lightly over Emily.

Emily lowered her eyes, feeling inexplicably guilty. James had fair skin and a red mole at the bottom of his right eye,which turned particularly red every time they were intimate.

Emily loved kissing that spot and seeing James with red eyes.

James was a fiery, intense ball of energy, with a sunny and innocent personality. His eyes under those slightly raised eyelids were always pure and innocent. When Emily was in a bad mood, James would do his best to cheer her up.When she was in the mood, he would let her do as she pleased. Then,when Emily begged for mercy with tears, he would plead for one more tíme. But now, he was aloof, with a noble demeanor. He was full of restraint,as if looking at him for a moment longer would be a blasphemy.

Emily thought it was just a coincidence that they looked alike, both had a red mole in their eyes and shared the same name.

She kept comforting herself this way, then lowered her head and stopped looking.

James went straight to the second floor. After he disappeared,Emily finally let out a long breath.

The people around her started discussing James again.

"James is really handsome. He must have been looking at me."

"No way, he was definitely looking at me."

Emily couldn't hear a word they were saying now.

James stood on the second floor, leaning on the railing, looking down with indifference.

Emily turned her head to look, and their eyes met again.

This time she didn't avoid it. Even though they were far apart,she could clearly feel James' sharp gaze like a hawk.

He couldn't be that James. She had just dumped James and even paid him to not bother her. She couldn't bear to think about it.

Emily lowered her head, looked at the message on her phone,and James still hadn't replied.

Emily didn't know where she got the courage, and sent: [I just saw someone who looks exactly like you. What a coincidence.] She turned to look. The James upstairs wasn't looking at his phone, nor did he reply. She was about to relax.

When James took out his phone, she quickly lowered her head,staring at Facebook.

James:[Is that so?]

Each word struck her heart.

Emily looked up again, James looked at his phone, then slowly raised his head,his gaze obscure.

James continued: [Such delicious coffee, won't you bring me a cup?]

Emily's grip on her phone tightened so much that it seemed like she might crush it.

Emily couldn't describe her feelings at the moment. All the lewd past events kept replaying in her mind.

She and James never had a serious relationship. James was her secret lover,the embodiment of her most wanton side.

Emily had always been a good girl, and having an affair with James was the most outrageous thing she had ever done.

She couldn't help but think of the scene when she first met James. On the day of the wedding, after John left, she didn't even change out of her wedding dress and took her friend Ella García to a bustling nightlife district. They arrived at the top club of the Emerald City, where the rich spend their gold. She lost John, but she had plenty of money.

With a wave, she generously bought out all the male waiters in the club for that night. When she went to the bathroom, she saw James wearing a waiter's uniform.

Emily drunkenly lifted James' chin.

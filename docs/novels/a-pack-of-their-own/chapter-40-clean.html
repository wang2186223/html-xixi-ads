"Something to do with the fact that it might be stopping the blood flow right now, and if we take it out without the proper medical procedure, it could let her bleed to death." “Oh shit!”“Yeah, oh shit is right."* I close the link and catch up to them in the waiting room area. About 5 minutes later, Mom and Dad come in.

How did she get stabbed?"“Lissa said that there were some warriors around her, and things were complete chaos when they announced the rogue attack, It may have been an accident, stemming from the chaos.” Liam states.(CARLA:“Liam, what are you doing here? DR. GRANT: “Alpha, Luna, your daughter is extremely lucky and very blessed."“Please explain, Dr.” The Alpha said. "The blade only punctured the human appendix, so no real vital organs were damaged, but we did have to remove the appendix. The part that makes her blessed is that the silver did not affect her, like a normal wolf. How or why, I cannot say. She will have to stay in the hospital overnight for observation, but then she will be able to go home. There will be restrictions for her for the next couple of months, because she does not have her wolf, she will have to heal like a normal human. No strenuous activities, she will be able to do stairs, albeit slowly, no heavy lifting of any kind, no overhead reaching for the first 2 wweeks. She will need to just take it easy, no long-distance traveling." I tell the family."May we go see her?" "Yes, but not all at once,and not for long, she needs to rest, to heal. She is in the Alpha wing.”“Thank you, Dr.”"You are welcome." I leave them to go about my rounds.

KESKA: I wake up in a strange place, with an annoying beeping going off in my left ear. "Hmm, make it stop." I mumble and I don't recognize my own voice. I slowly open my eyes. "No, too bright.” I mumble again.It came out like a croak of a frog. Where in the goddess's name am I. I hear movement around me. I can feel the light dim. I try to open my eyes again. I am in a room; it's a light beige color, and it smells like disinfectant."Where am I?”"In the hospital." I hear a familiar voice.“Nana?”"Yes, dear.""How did I get here?""I had Stephen carry you."“What happened?”“You were stabbed, dear.""What? Wait, Rogues, Nana,Lissa, is she ok?" I say as panic sets in. "Calm down, dear, Lissa is just fine, there were no rogues, it was a false alarm." She tells me. I try to get up,but there is pain on the right side of my stomach. "Ahhhggg." “Kes,

stop moving, or you will tear your stitches." She tells me.“What happened?Why am I here? And why does my stomach hurt?”“Well,we were hoping you could tell us what happened. What do you remember?"“Someone called out Rogues at the North border. Frank told me to get Lissa and get to the safe room. I linked her to run to the pack house, and I would be right behind her. Buat someone grabbed me, they put their hand over my mouth. I felt the knife go into my right thigh, then I think I was picked up by my throat, I kicked him, but then I got stabbed in the side,and everything went dark after that." It was a bit fuzzy, but it was coming back to me. “Do you think the attack was targeted, or an accident?"“What do you mean?"“Do you think the attack that happened was deliberately meant for you?" “I don't know Nana, maybe. There was so much chaos, it's hard to tell."“Ok, sweetheart, don't stress over it, get some rest, the doctor says you will be here overnight." "What time is it?”“Almost dinner time."“Love you, Nana.”“Love you too,rest." She leaves the room. Then Father and Mom come in. "Oh, my baby!” Mom is in tears."Drake,we can't leave now.""What are you talking about, Mom?”"The trip to High Crest and Dutch Wood. We can't leave you here like this.”“Oh! Hell no! You will go, I am not going to get blamed for Jessie not getting to go!" I almost yell. “Calm down, Keska, we don't want you tearing your stitches. And watch your language and tone, young lady!" Father states. I bow my head and bare my neck. I link James, *"You had better convince Mom and Father to take you, Jessie, Wyatt, and Star on that trip!”“What are you on about?""The graduation trip, I am not getting blamed for Jessie not getting to go! So, whatever you have to do to convince them, you better do it!"* I close the link. "Who were you just talking to?" “James." Father just shakes his head, he knows what I did, I'm sure. "What would you like for dinner?" Father asks me. "Whatever they are serving, I don't need a special meal.""Well, actually, you do have to eat soft foods for a couple of days, so you don't stress the internal stitches." Came a new voice. “What do you mean. Dr?" "We had to put a few stitches in place when we took

the appendix out. So soft foods, like mashed potatoes with gravy,mashed up hamburger with gravy, meat loaf with gravy, soft veggies, no lettuce salad. No stake yet, understand?”

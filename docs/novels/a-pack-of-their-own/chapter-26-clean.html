I said to her.“That's because I didn't need one, and I am paying for it myself." She tells me with no emotion in her voice. "How are you paying forit?”“I have been doing odd jobs around the pack for years and have gotten paid for them.” Again, she answers in my direction without eye contact or much emotion.

LIAM: "Cool, can I come watch? Maybe I might want to take it to if I see how it's done." I say to her. "I will have to ask the Master." *(Liam, there is something you must understand, it is in the human world, not ours,that is why I didn't say anything about it, I know Father wouldn't have

Alpha."* (I am going to have to put someone on Kes just to find out what she does, where she goes; she clearly is not going to tell me anything by asking her.)

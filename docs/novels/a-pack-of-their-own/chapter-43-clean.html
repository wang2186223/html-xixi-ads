"Of course, I am not like Jessie and need to have my way.”

Just rest," They all say.

I just let my eyes close, and I am out.

LISSA: I link Dad. *"Daddy Kes is falling asleep on the couch in the game room."“I'll be right there, sweetie."* I close the link. In a couple of minutes. Dad comes and picks Kes up and takes her upstairs to her room to rest.

LIAM: "Hey Mackie?" "Yes, Liam?” “Can we go to the range and shoot while she sleeps? I will get some cash from Mom to pay for the rental."“You don't have to do that, I have enough to pay for the rental."“Between the two of you. you have always paid, I haven't made enough yet to pay.but I will soon, but Mom will give me enough to pay this time.

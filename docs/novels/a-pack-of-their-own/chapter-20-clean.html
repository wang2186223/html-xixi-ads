I just want to go eat lunch, and Jessie is griping about me ignoring her. I did not get her link."“Well,try to link her and see what happens." "James, I don't have time for all this nonsense. I did not get it."“Do you think it might have something to do with what happened a month ago?"“Well,if it does, then that's on her as well, and she threatened to tell mom and father,won't that go over just great. give her enough rope, but of course I know that I will get blamed for that too." "Let's go to class and forget about all this until it becomes an issue." She says.

Chapter 95

After Michael carried Isabella into the car, the familiar scent made her feel safe. The drug's effect on her was like a dam breaking,and she couldn't control the desire surging through her.

She pushed Michael down, moaning, her full lips exploring his body and her hands tearing at his clothes. In no time, Michael's neat suit was disarray, thanks to Isabella. Michael, typically the epitome of composure,was now clearly breathing heavily.

Jerry had raised the partition early on, but Isabella's wild moans from the backseat were impossible to ignore. Taking advantage of a red light, Jerry put on his headphones and played a passage from the Bible.

Jerry drove to the nearest hotel to the Taylor Villa. Aaron had already booked a room and was waiting there with Terry.

Terry took a glass that Aaron had prepared, sniffed it lightly,dipped his finger in, and then licked his finger to taste it.

Aaron found it strange. Other doctors were all about cleanliness and precision, but Terry was so different.

Terry said, "It's just a common aphrodisiac. Find a wwoman for Michael.Let him sleep with her, and he'll be fine. If he doesn't want to sleep with a woman, a cold bath with some ice cubes will also work."

Aaron asked, "What if a woman took the drug?"

Terry replied, "For a woman, I don't recommend a cold bath. From a medical perspective, it's too harmful to a woman's body. It's better to find a man for her. There are no side effects. It'll provide quick relief, and she will experience a pleasurable sensation. It's a win-win. Aaron, is there a woman you can't handle? Let me tell you, don't use such underhanded methods. Come to me, and I'll give you some tips to win her over..."

Terry was in the middle of his enthusiastic speech when Michael walked in carrying Isabella. Terry was stunned and pointed at Michael, asking Aaron, "What's... going on with him?"

.Aaron shrugged, indicating he couldn't tell him.

Terry,frustrated, poked Aaron several times with his finger.

"She was drugged. Check her quickly to see if it's serious and if she needs her stomach pumped," Michael said urgently.

"Aaron already checked the glass she used. It's just a common aphrodisiac.Find a man for her, Let her sleep with him, and she'll be fine," Terry said calmly while treating the wound on Isabella's leg. "If you can't find a man,let her take a cold bath. But for a girl, a cold bath is too harmful. I can help you..."

Terry was about to tell Michael that he could help him find one. After all,as a doctor, he had access to such "remedies." But before he could finish,

Michael grabbed him by the collar and threw him out.

Aaron and Jerry stood guard at the door.

"What the hell is going on? Is Michael going to be the remedy for that woman himself?" he asked Aaron and Jerry.

Aaron and Jerry, as if they had agreed beforehand, responded with silence.

Seeing he couldn't get anything out of them, Terry called Jeremy and said,"Jeremy, I've got some shocking news for you. You won't believe what I found out."

"Calm down and spill it," Jeremy said, used to Terry's dramatic flair.

"Jeremy,brace yourself. Don't fall off your chair," Terry said mysteriously. "

Michael, he's not gay. He's with a woman! I caught him!"

Terry laughed triumphantly, but Jeremy just frowned, unimpressed. "That's your big news? You didn't know Michael being gay was a lie? He's had a woman for a long time."

"Oh," Terry's excitement deflated. "I saw Michael bring a woman to a hotel and thought it was big news. Turns out you all kept it from me. Jeremy, I'm hurt."

Jeremy didn't care about his feelings. He only heard the part about Michael bringing a woman to a hotel.

"Hurry up and tell me which hotel and the room number. I'm going to catch them in the act," Jeremy said excitedly.

"Jeremy, spare me. Michael will kill me if he finds out. Aaron and Jerry are there. Ask them," Terry said, hanging up.

Jeremy immediately called Terry back. "If I could get anything out of them,would I be wasting time with you? Come on, tell me. I promise I won't say anything. Don't you want to know Michael's private affairs? Let me tell you, such opportunities don't come every day."

Terry's desire to see the drama was so strong that, with Jeremy's promise,he sent the address without hesitation.

Looking at the address on his phone, Jeremy couldn't help but marvel at how desperate Michael must be to choose such a remote place.

With the adIdress in hand, Jeremy hurried to find Qiana and told her about Michael booking a room at a remote hotel with a woman.

Qiana was furious when she heard this. She slammed her hand on the table, not even noticing the pain, and Jeremy quickly moved to comfort her.

"Michael swore to me that he truly loved Isabella. It's only been a few days,and he's already entangled with another woman. He's so outrageous. HIow

is he going to explain this to Isabella?" Qiana said through gritted teeth,looking like she could tear a piece off Michael if he were in front of her.

"Qiana,should we bring Isabella along?" Jeremy suggested, eager for more .drama.

Qiana sighed and said, "Isabella is a good girl. If MMichael didn't have someone else in his heart, I would think they wwere a perfect match. But Michael... it's just not fair to Isabella. Now that Michael has caused such a scene, she would be heartbroken if she found out. Let's not involve her.We'll try to get rid of the woman Michael is with."

Jeremy tried to comfort her, "Don't worry about Michael. He's an adult and has his status. Even if he doesn't make a move, women throw themselves at him. Men like me, who are devoted, faithful, and loyal,are almost extinct."

Qiana gave him a disdainful look and rolled her eyes. She walked out, and Jeremy followed, carrying her bag, acting as her chauffeur, and driving to their destination.
